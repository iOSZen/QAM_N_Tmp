Encryption & Decryption

Type a string, choose a method (default: XOR), encrypt it and paste encrypted text back to decrypt. Safe for quick obfuscation — not for high-security secrets.

Tip: Paste the encrypted string into the "Encrypted" box to decrypt directly.
Methods: Base64 — encoding (Unicode-safe), XOR — bytewise XOR with numeric key, result base64-encoded, Caesar — simple char-shift (not for Unicode-sensitive use).
Created with ❤️ — quick obfuscation tools. Not a replacement for real cryptography.
